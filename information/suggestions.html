Filtering Transportation - Suggestions

You can make a suggestion for either our Faction in-game as well as our Discord server! You can do this by doing the following:

  • If you want to make an in-game suggestion, use ft/fsuggestion [suggestion].

  • If you want to make an Discord server suggestion, use ft/dsuggestion [suggestion].

Once you make your suggestion, it will send an embed in the #suggestions channel, and in this channel you can upvote, downvote and facepalm on a suggestion!
