Filtering Transportation - Faction and Discord size channels

In the Filtering Transportation's Discord server, there are 2 channels with a number, which are:

  • Discord Size: xx
  • Faction Size: xx

The Discord Size channel is how many people there are with the Guest role. This role gets assigned to you once you verify yourself. This channel gets updated once every hour.

The Faction Size channel is how many people there are currently in the Transport Tycoon faction. This checks the amount of people using the Transport Tycoon API. This channel gets updated once every 3 hours!
